Team Splitting

(A) Split into Two Teams of Six

For each pair of twins, there are two ways to split them into two teams. Since there are 6 pairs, the total number of splits is:
2^6 = 64
Since the teams are unlabelled (swapping teams counts as the same division), we divide by 2:
64 ÷ 2 = 32

(B) Split into Three Teams of Four

Step 1: First, select 4 pupils by picking one out of 4 pairs, which are chosen from 6 available pairs:
6C4 * 2^4 = 15 * 16 = 240
Step 2: After that, two pairs remain untouched, and four pairs already have one twin assigned. We must select one twin from the two untouched pairs to avoid having two twins together in the last team:
4C2 * (2C2 * 2^2) = 6 * (1 * 4) = 24
Step 3: The last 4 pupils automatically form the last team — no further choice needed:
1 way
Total for labelled teams:
6C4 * 2^4 * 4C2 * (2C2 * 2^2) = 15 * 16 * 6 * (1 * 4) = 5760
Final Adjustment for Unlabelled Teams: Since the teams are unlabelled, divide by 3! to avoid overcounting:
5760 ÷ 6 = 960
