Team Splitting

(A) Split into Two Teams of Six

For each pair of twins, there are two ways to split them into two teams. Since there are 6 pairs, the total number of splits is:
2^6 = 64

Since the teams are unlabelled (swapping teams counts as the same division), we divide by 2:
64 รท 2 = 32

(B) Split into Three Teams of Four

First, select 4 pairs from 6 pairs. From each pair, select one twin:
6C4 * 2^4

Afterward, we are forced to select one twin from the two untouched pairs to avoid placing both twins on the same team:
4C2 * (2C2 * 2^2)

The last 4 people automatically form the last team:
1 way

Total for labelled teams:
6C4 * 2^4 * 4C2 * (2C2 * 2^2) = 5760

Since teams are unlabelled, divide by 3!:
5760 รท 6 = 960
